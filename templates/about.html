Frequently Asked Questions

Is any investment required from the hospital?

No. Medvika covers 100% of setup costs, licensing, staffing, and operations. You invest zero and earn revenue share.

How is revenue share calculated?

Revenue share is calculated on net profit (after all expenses). Hospital receives 60% while Medvika retains 40% for operations and management.

Who handles pharmacy licensing and staff?

Medvika handles all licensing, staff recruitment, training, and compliance. We ensure full regulatory adherence.

How is daily reporting managed?

We provide daily sales, revenue, and inventory reports. Hospital receives transparent, audit-ready records daily.

Is this a long-term partnership?

Yes. We believe in sustainable partnerships. Once established, we work together for long-term mutual growth.
